About the Community Forest

Since the 1990s, Campbell's Bay Primary School has nurtured a regenerating native forest - a living classroom where students connect with New Zealand's natural heritage. What began as overgrown land is now home to tūī, kererū, and thriving native species.

These resources were designed to make the forest accessible to everyone - children discovering nature, families exploring together, and visitors seeking connection to our local ecology. Through field guides, this website, and on - site wayfinding, we invite you to walk our tracks, learn about native species, and become part of our forest's continuing story.
